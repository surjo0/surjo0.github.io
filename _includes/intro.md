Hi! I am Surjo, a recent graduate from the Department of Computer Science at **Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Petroleum Technology**. I completed my Secondary and Higher Secondary education in Bangladesh before pursuing my bachelor's degree in India. I am currently planning to pursue a PhD in the areas of Computer Vision, Vision-Language Models, and Generative AI.

During my bachelor's degree, I worked with Prof. [Susham Biswas](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=l1T71wcAAAAJ&hl=en) on research focused on structural deformation identification and analysis using machine learning. I also worked with Dr. [Pallabi Saikia](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=JmNyqQUAAAAJ&hl=en) on theoretical and computational analysis of convergence and explainability in diffusion-based generative models for Vision AI.

Currently, I am working on Vision-Language Models under the supervision of Dr. [Vinay Kumar Verma](https://scholar.google.com/citations?hl=en&user=7x6GZ1EAAAAJ) (Senior Applied Scientist at Amazon, formerly Duke University, USA) and Dr. [Swalpa Kumar Roy](https://scholar.google.com/citations?hl=en&user=1WVrFGwAAAAJ) (Associate Professor, Tezpur University).
